Car Rental System involves with modules, user management, car inventory, rental booking, and rental administration. In the user management module, the system should support user registration and login. Customer can only register and login. Admins will have full control over car records, including the ability to add, update, and delete entries in the car database, which stores data such as ID, make, model, year, mileage, availability status, and rental period constraints. Customers can browse the list of available cars along with their details and make rental bookings by selecting a vehicle, specifying rental dates, and submitting the required information. Then the system calculate rental fees for the chosen vehicle, rental duration. Admins can  approve or reject rental requests as required.
